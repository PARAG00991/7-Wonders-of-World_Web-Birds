    Key Points

  • Located in Cusco Region, Urubamba province, Machupicchu district in Peru
  • Machu Picchu means "OLD MOUNTAINS"
  • In 15th century,the Incan Emperor Pachacutec built a city in the clouds on the mountain known as Machu Picchu
  • Rediscovered by American historian, Hiram Bingham in 1911
