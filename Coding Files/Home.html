7 WONDERS OF WORLD

The Official New 7 Wonders of the World have been elected by more than 100 million votes to represent global heritage throughout history. The listing is in random order, as announced at the Declaration Ceremony on 07.07.07.

All The Official New 7 Wonders of the World are equal and are presented as a group without any ranking.

The most recent 7 Wonders of the World are shown below:

Taj Mahal: Agra, India Christ Redeemer: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Roman Colosseum: Rome, Italy Machu Picchu: Peru Great Wall of China: China
